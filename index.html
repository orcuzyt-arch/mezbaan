Our Story

Authentic flavors,
reimagined for the Kingdom.

We started Mezbaan Sweets & Chaat with a simple vision: to create a premium, uncompromised experience of India's vibrant street food and rich traditional sweets.

Every dish is crafted with generations-old recipes, modern finesse, and a commitment to quality that you can taste in every bite — from the first crunch of a kachori to the last spoonful of rabri malai.
